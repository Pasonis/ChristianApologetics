Grace be with you, mercy, and peace, from God the Father, and from

I rejoiced greatly that I found of thy children walking in truth,

And now I beseech thee, lady, not as though I wrote a new
commandment unto thee, but that which we had from the beginning, that
we love one another.<a id="kjv-2-john-1-5"></a><sup>5</sup>

And this is love, that we walk after his commandments. This is the
commandment, That, as ye have heard from the beginning, ye should walk
in it.<a id="kjv-2-john-1-6"></a><sup>6</sup>

For many deceivers are entered into the world, who confess not
that Jesus Christ is come in the flesh. This is a deceiver and an
antichrist.<a id="kjv-2-john-1-7"></a><sup>7</sup>

Look to yourselves, that we lose not those things which we have
wrought, but that we receive a full reward.<a id="kjv-2-john-1-8"></a><sup>8</sup>

Whosoever transgresseth, and abideth not in the doctrine of
Christ, hath not God. He that abideth in the doctrine of Christ, he
hath both the Father and the Son.<a id="kjv-2-john-1-9"></a><sup>9</sup>

If there come any unto you, and bring not this doctrine, receive
him not into your house, neither bid him God speed:<a id="kjv-2-john-1-10"></a><sup>10</sup> For he that

Having many things to write unto you, I would not write with
