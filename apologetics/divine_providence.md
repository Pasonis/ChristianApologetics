# Divine Providence
God's active, wise and loving goverance of creation, whereby He sustains all things, directs history toward salvation, and works within human freedom without abolishing it.[1](#div-prov-ref-1).

## Foreknowledge is not causation
1. Two wills
	1. Perfect will - What God Desires in accordance with His goodness
	1. Permissive will - What God allows because he respects freedom [1](#div-prov-ref-1)
